What is MUN?

  1. MUN stands for model united nations. Model United Nations is a simulation of United Nations (UN)
  2. MUN offers students an opportunity to experience to be a representative of a particular country (delegate) at UN.
  3. The UN website describes a MUN conference as an authentic simulation of the UN General Assembly and other multilateral bodies.

Why participate in MUN?

  1. MUN not only helps you get into college but provides you with a rich learning experience.
  2. It also builds leadership skills and public speaking skills, negotiation and much more!
  3. It also teaches how to research in depth on a given topic and then present it.
  4. It helps you learn about diplomacy, international relations, current world issues.
