General speakers’ list :
• MUN language - GSL

• Used to discuss agenda in general to get the stance of every speaker
• Can be reintroduced at any point of debate

SPECIFIES
 Time allotted per speaker: usually no more than 60 to 90 sec. o Number of speaker allotted: Whoever wishes to speak
 No topic statements
• way to begin committee or get committee’s stance on a certain issue
• Usually found as boring/mundane by EB/Delegates

How to begin (if the floor for motions is open):
 Step 1 – Rise your placard, and wait to be addressed by the EB.
 Step 2 – And then say – MOTION TO ENTER INTO GSL
