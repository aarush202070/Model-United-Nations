Terms



  1. Executive Board(EB):the executive board of a mun is comprised of a group of people who have been selected after a long process of judging their skills andexperience, because they are going to be entrusted with one of the biggest tasks in making an MUN conference successful - to guide their committee through debate on the agenda and throughout the debate, to judge the delegates.

  2. Delegate: A participant in a MUN conference is called a delegate, he ore she represents a particular country at a particular committee

  3. Placard:A placard is a signboard that signifies a delegate’s country. It is generally used for voting and to show a delegate’s prescence

  4. Position paper: A mun position paper also known as a policy paper is a strategic document that gives an overview of a delegate’s country position .

  5. Points:


    6. • Point of personal privilege-can be raised as a personal request to the EB

    Example:point of personal privilege to increase the temperature of the AC

    • Point of parliamentary enquiry-can be raised when the delegate has a question regarding the functioning of the committee

    Example: point of parliamentary enquiry when does the session end?


    • Point of information-can be raised during the GSL and during PSL speeches if the EB allows.


    • Point of order-It is of two types and can be raised on all speeches.:


    • Factual inaccuracy- It can be raised in case that the delegate has sited the wrong information in another delegate’s speech.

    • Logical fallacy-It can be raised in case a delegate has made a statement that is logically incorrect.


  6. Motion-A motion is a formal way that a delegate can put in an action.

  7. GSL- GSL stands for general speakers list and you will learn about it in the upcoming pages

  8. Moderated caucus-Moderated caucus is a type of motion which will be explained in the upcoming pages

  9. unmoderated caucus is a type of motion which will be explained in the upcoming pages

  10. Draft resolution-A draft resolution indicates the end of the committee and explains solutions discussed in the various committee sessions.
