What is Position Paper?

A mun position paper also known as a policy paper is a strategic document that gives an overview of a delegate’s country position .
A good position paper has three parts

What are the types?

•Country’s position on the topic
• Country’s relation to the topic
• Proposals of policies to pass a resolution (solutions)

How to make a good Position Paper?

A solid position paper won't just give the facts, but also suggest some resolutions. your Position Paper needs to be full of new solutions
Points for writing a good position paper:
• Define your stance
• Explain your country’s relation to the agenda a don’t be vague
• Write proposals and solutions
• Country proposal
• Be emotional about what you believe in

Format


Introduction: Introduce the topic and your position on the topic
Body: This section contains your arguments or claims with supporting evidence.
Conclusion: The final section of a position paper emphasizes your claims, summarizes your paper and mainly suggests solutions
